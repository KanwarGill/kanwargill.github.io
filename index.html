About

Kanwar is a respected Engineering Leader who brings over seven years of extensive experience in the e-commerce and banking industries. With a strong background in various technical domains, Kanwar's expertise encompasses Full-Stack Development, Mathematics, Computer Science, Microservices, Serverless Architectures, Systems Programming, Scripting, and AWS. As a skilled public speaker, Kanwar has also been an active participant in Toastmasters International.

Kanwar holds a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the esteemed University of Toronto and has consistently demonstrated professionalism and dedication to excellence. In addition, Kanwar has earned a Master's degree in Teaching with a focus on Mathematics and Computer Science from the Ontario Institute for Studies in Education (OISE). As an Ontario Certified Teacher (OCT), Kanwar is passionate about fostering growth and learning in others.

Throughout Kanwar's career, SOLID engineering principles have been upheld to ensure high-quality and maintainable solutions for the organizations they have had the privilege of working with. Kanwar is eager to continue leveraging their experience and knowledge to drive success and innovation in the industry.
